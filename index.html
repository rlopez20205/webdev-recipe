Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of chocolate ice cream
  • 1 cup of chocolate milk
  • 2 tablespoon chocolate syrup, plus more for serving
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • sweetened whipped cream

Methods

  1. process ice cream
  2. proccess choclate milk
  3. process chocolate syrup
  4. process vanilla
  5. pour all into the blender
  6. put blender on a smooth high
  7. top it off with whipcream
  8. final touch is some more choclate syrup
